# Product

## Register
brand

## Product Name
Desert Fathers Studio

## Product Purpose
An e-commerce storefront selling Coptic Orthodox icon stickers — hand-designed sacred art for everyday life (Bibles, planners, water bottles). Also serves a wholesale portal for Sunday schools and parishes, and an admin dashboard for the owner.

## Users
- **D2C**: Coptic Orthodox Christians in the US, ages 15–50. Faith is central to their identity. They value beauty, authenticity, and the sacred. Not overly brand-conscious but highly taste-conscious when it comes to religious art.
- **Wholesale**: Sunday school teachers, youth pastors, and parish deacons ordering in bulk (60+ stickers at a time) for classes and events.
- **Admin**: Jerome (the owner). Solo operator, highly familiar with the product.

## Tone
Reverent, warm, honest. Not preachy. Not corporate. Not folksy. Like a well-curated religious bookshop — quiet authority, genuine faith.

## Brand Values
- Sacred art deserves to travel with you, not just hang in a church
- Coptic tradition is ancient, global, and beautiful
- Accessible price point ($0.70/sticker) without feeling cheap

## Anti-references
- NO purple/blue AI gradients
- NO emoji in headings
- NO generic SaaS look (white background + blue CTA)
- NO 3-column icon grids
- NO comic-sans or craft-fair aesthetic
- NO stock photography

## Strategic Principles
- Editorial and gallery-like — stickers deserve space to breathe
- Warm, not cold. Cream/off-white backgrounds, maroon accent, gold highlights
- Serif headings with personality, clean sans-serif body
- Motion should feel reverent, not playful
